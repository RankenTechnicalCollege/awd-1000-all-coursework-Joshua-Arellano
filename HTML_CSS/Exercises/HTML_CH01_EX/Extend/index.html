Chapter 1, Extend your Knowledge

Guideline 1.1 of the WCAG 2 is to provide text alternatives if their is any non text content, such as images or videos.

Guideline 1.2 explains to have an alternative to text, audio, and video content if their is only text, audio, or video content and vice versa. Some examples being, captions for a video, transcripts for audio or video content, and even sign language for audio content.

Guideline 1.3 explains to create content that can be presented in different ways without losing meaning such as, using headings and lists to organize information, able to read in any orientation without losing information.

Guideline 1.4 shows to make content easier to read and hear by using visible colors to read, resize text, text spacing, and pausable audio content.
